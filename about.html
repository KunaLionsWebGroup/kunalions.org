The Kuna Lions Club, a non-profit service oriented organization, was founded in 1967 by a group of community businessmen who had a sincere desire to serve in our local community. The newly formed Kuna Lions Club immediately set out to serve and completed numerous community projects, invested thousands of hours and, through hard work, honesty, honor in business and commitment to our community, grew the Kuna Lions Club into one of the most recognized and successful service organizations in the Kuna area. You can make a difference!
